## Notes for Future Development

- **India-first advantage**: Tools like GST, EPF, HRA, Salary Slip, Old vs New Tax have very low competition in the "free + no signup" segment.
- **Privacy angle**: Market the fact that everything runs in-browser (big differentiator vs many online calculators).
- **Bundle potential**: Create "Finance Toolkit", "Developer Toolkit", "Content Creator Toolkit" pages.
- **Monetization**: High-intent finance tools (loan eligibility, tax) tend to convert better for AdSense.
